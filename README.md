# Grenius

## About the project
- The Grenius project is an online platform inspired by the popular Genius service, which provides the user with access to a wide range of musical texts, as well as information about musicians, albums and songs. The main goal of Genius is to help people understand song lyrics more deeply by exploring their meanings, interpretations and context.
- On the Grenius platform, users can find the lyrics of their favorite songs, as well as view annotations and comments added by other users or by the artists themselves. In addition, Grenius provides information on music news, interviews with artists, interview texts, reviews and more to create an extensive music community and an environment for sharing knowledge and ideas about music.
- Thanks to Grenius, users have the opportunity to expand their knowledge of the music world, delve into the work of their favorite artists and discuss their work with other like-minded people.
